FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I activate a bundle?
After purchase, your bundle activates instantly on your registered number. You'll receive an SMS confirmation within seconds.
Can I buy a bundle for someone else?
Yes! During checkout, simply enter the recipient's phone number instead of yours. Gift data to family and friends instantly.
What happens when my data runs out?
You'll receive an SMS alert when you're at 20% remaining. Once data is exhausted, you can top up instantly — your number stays active.
Do bundles roll over to the next period?
Monthly bundles carry up to 20% unused data to the next month if you renew before expiry. Daily and weekly bundles do not roll over.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money), bank cards (Visa/Mastercard), and cash at any of our agent locations.
